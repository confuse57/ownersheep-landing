How to Delete Your Prompt Data from Ownersheep If you have submitted any ideas (prompts) on Ownersheep, you can delete them from your “My Prompt” section inside the app. Open the app Go to My Prompt Long-press or tap the delete icon on the prompt you want to remove ⚠️ No account deletion is required to remove your submitted prompts. Types of data deleted: Prompt content only Data retention policy: Deleted data is permanently removed immediately and not retained.
