Data Deletion Policy

Last updated: 25 July 2025

How to Delete Your Prompt Data from Ownersheep
  • Open the Ownersheep app
  • Go to My Prompt
  • Long-press or tap the delete icon on the prompt you want to remove
⚠️ No account deletion is required to remove your submitted prompts.
Types of data deleted: Prompt content only
Data retention policy: Deleted data is permanently removed immediately and not retained.
For further assistance, contact us at notify@ownersheep.app.
