Intellectual Property Policy

Last updated: July 25, 2025

1. Overview

This Intellectual Property Policy outlines how Ownersheep handles, protects, and respects intellectual property rights on our platform. We are committed to fostering innovation while protecting the rights of creators and inventors.

Your Ideas Remain Yours: We fundamentally believe that your intellectual property belongs to you. Our platform exists to help you understand and maximize the value of your ideas, not to claim ownership over them.

2. User-Submitted Content Ownership

Your Intellectual Property Rights

When you submit ideas, concepts, or any other content to Ownersheep:

  • You retain full ownership of all intellectual property rights
  • We do not claim ownership of your ideas or innovations
  • Your content remains confidential unless you choose to make it public
  • You control sharing permissions and can revoke access at any time

Limited License Grant

To provide our services, you grant Ownersheep a limited, non-exclusive, revocable license to:

  • Analyze your submitted content using our AI systems
  • Generate valuation reports and market insights
  • Store and process your data securely
  • Provide you with analysis results and recommendations

3. AI Analysis and IP Protection

Secure Processing

Our AI analysis systems are designed with IP protection in mind:

  • Encrypted data processing ensures your ideas remain confidential
  • Isolated analysis environments prevent data leakage between users
  • No cross-contamination of ideas or analysis results
  • Automated data purging after analysis completion

Anonymized Learning

We may use anonymized, non-identifiable data patterns to improve our AI models, but:

  • No specific ideas or concepts are retained
  • Only general market trends and patterns are analyzed
  • Your identity and specific content remain completely confidential
  • You can opt out of this anonymized data usage at any time

4. Copyright and Trademark Protection

DMCA Compliance

We respect copyright laws and maintain a DMCA-compliant process:

  • Prompt response to valid copyright infringement claims
  • Clear process for submitting takedown notices
  • Counter-notification procedures for disputed claims
  • Account termination for repeat infringers

Trademark Respect

We do not tolerate trademark infringement on our platform:

  • Users must not submit content that infringes on existing trademarks
  • Clear reporting mechanism for trademark violations
  • Swift action against infringing content

5. Patent Considerations

Important: Using our platform to analyze your ideas does not constitute public disclosure that would affect your patent rights. Our analysis is conducted under confidentiality.

Patent Prior Art Searches

Our AI systems may reference public patent databases to provide comprehensive analysis:

  • Only publicly available patent information is used
  • Analysis helps identify potential patent conflicts
  • Recommendations for patent research and filing
  • No guarantee of patent approval or validity

Maintaining Patent Rights

To preserve your patent rights:

  • Avoid public disclosure of your ideas outside our platform
  • Consider filing provisional patents before extensive market analysis
  • Consult with patent attorneys for specific legal advice
  • Use our confidentiality features to protect sensitive information

6. Trade Secrets Protection

We understand the importance of trade secrets and implement robust protection measures:

  • Strict confidentiality agreements with all employees and contractors
  • Limited access controls ensuring only authorized personnel can access data
  • Secure data storage with encryption and access logging
  • Regular security audits to maintain protection standards

7. Future IP Marketplace

Voluntary Participation

Our planned IP marketplace will operate on voluntary participation:

  • You choose which ideas to list for potential licensing or sale
  • You set your own terms and pricing
  • We facilitate connections but do not force transactions
  • You maintain control over your IP throughout the process

Transaction Protection

When marketplace features launch, we will provide:

  • Secure escrow services for financial transactions
  • Legal documentation templates for IP transfers
  • Dispute resolution mechanisms
  • Verification processes for serious buyers and sellers

If you believe your intellectual property rights have been violated:

9. Our Intellectual Property

While we respect your IP rights, we also maintain rights to our platform and technologies:

  • Platform technology and user interface designs
  • AI algorithms and analysis methodologies
  • Proprietary databases and market intelligence systems
  • Brand elements including logos, trademarks, and content

10. Data Retention and Deletion

You have control over your data and IP on our platform:

  • Account deletion: Removes all your submitted content
  • Selective deletion: Remove specific ideas or projects
  • Data export: Download your data before deletion
  • Secure deletion: Complete removal from our systems within 30 days
