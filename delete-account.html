Delete Your Ownersheep Account

How to Permanently Delete Your Account
  • Open the Ownersheep app
  • Go to Profile > Settings
  • Tap on Delete My Account
  • Confirm the deletion
What will be deleted:
  • All your submitted prompts
  • All comments, likes, followers
  • Your account login and profile info
For further assistance, contact us at notify@ownersheep.app.
