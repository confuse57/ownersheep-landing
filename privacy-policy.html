1. Information We Collect

When you use Ownersheep, we may collect the following personal information:

We automatically collect information about how you interact with our platform:

AI Analysis: Your submitted ideas undergo AI-powered analysis to determine market potential, feasibility, and valuation. This processing is conducted securely and in accordance with industry standards.

Our AI systems analyze:

3. Data Protection and Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive information
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Restricted access controls and employee training
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with backup systems

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent for specific purposes
  • To comply with legal obligations or law enforcement requests
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With trusted service providers who assist in platform operations

5. Your Rights and Controls

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Transfer your data to another service
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data

6. Cookie Policy

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to:
