Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 July 2025

Ownersheep is a sub-brand of Tagbudy Services Private Limited

Welcome to Ownersheep! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform, services, and website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, address, submitted content, idea information, profile biography, media uploads, and documents (if any).
  • Account Information: Login credentials, account preferences, and settings.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, device information, IP address, browser type.
  • Cookies & Tracking: Cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies for analytics and personalization.
  • Communication Data: Messages, feedback, support requests, and correspondence.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain Ownersheep services.
  • To personalize your experience and improve our platform.
  • To communicate with you regarding updates, offers, and support.
  • To analyze usage and trends to enhance functionality.
  • To ensure security and prevent fraud.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing Your Information

  • With service providers and partners who assist in operating Ownersheep (under confidentiality agreements).
  • With law enforcement or regulatory authorities when required by law.
  • During business transfers (merger, acquisition, sale of assets).
  • With your consent or as otherwise disclosed at the time of collection.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Access to data is limited to authorized personnel only. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Storage Location

Your data is stored securely on cloud services such as AWS/Google Cloud with data centers located in India and/or other regions as required for platform functionality.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Ownersheep uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and deliver personalized content. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

7. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.
  • Request restriction or objection to certain data processing.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

8. Children's Privacy

Ownersheep does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us for removal.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated date. Please review periodically.

10. Intellectual Property Protection

Ownersheep allows users to submit original ideas, projects, and content, which may carry intellectual value. We do not claim ownership of your submitted content. However, by submitting content, you grant Ownersheep a limited, non-exclusive license to display and store the content for platform functionality.
We implement technical and procedural safeguards to prevent unauthorized copying, sharing, or misuse of submitted content. However, please note that we do not offer legal IP registration, and recommend users consult appropriate legal authorities for formal IP rights.

11. Content Visibility & User Responsibility

Users are advised not to share confidential or highly sensitive ideas unless they are comfortable with the visibility level. While we implement measures to prevent plagiarism and copying, Ownersheep cannot guarantee absolute protection against idea imitation.
We offer features like timestamping, ownership certificates, and content logs to help demonstrate originality in case of disputes.

12. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data anytime by contacting us.
